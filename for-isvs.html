02 · ISV questions

How Blacfox works with ISVs.

What is channel enablement for ISVs?

Channel enablement for ISVs is the process of equipping partner resellers and implementers with the positioning, messaging, sales assets, and training they need to sell an ISV's product effectively. Effective ISV channel enablement means partners can explain what the software does, who it's for, why it's different from competitors, and how to handle common objections — without the ISV being in the room.

How does Blacfox help ISVs generate demand through their partner channel?

Blacfox works with ISVs in two motions simultaneously: demand generation at the end-customer level (so buyers are asking for your product by name) and channel enablement at the partner level (so partners are actively selling it and positioned correctly). The two motions reinforce each other — end-customer pull makes your product easier for partners to sell, and partner-led positioning makes campaigns more credible to end-buyers.
