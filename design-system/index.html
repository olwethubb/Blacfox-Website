Foundation

Logos

Two brand assets — the fox icon mark and the full wordmark. The icon is used in navigation and compact placements; the wordmark appears on social ads, email templates, and ebooks.

Fox Icon
Standalone mark — navigation, favicons, and small-format placements
On Dark — 64 / 40 / 24px
On Orange — 64 / 40 / 24px
Full Wordmark
Primary brand logo — social ads, email headers, and ebooks
Usage Guidelines
Social Ads
Use the full wordmark (white on dark or black on orange). Never place on a mid-grey background.
Email Headers
Use the full wordmark (white on dark) in the email header. The fox icon alone is acceptable in the email footer at 24px.
eBooks / PDFs
Full wordmark on cover and footer of each page. Minimum display width: 200px.

Foundation

Animations

Scroll-triggered reveal classes. Applied to elements; the JS IntersectionObserver adds .visible when in view.

.reveal
Fade + slide up (Y+40px)
.reveal-left
Fade + slide from left (X-40px)
.reveal-right
Fade + slide from right (X+40px)
.reveal-scale
Fade + scale from 0.94
.stagger
Children stagger 0.08s each (up to 8)
transition
opacity 0.7s ease, transform 0.7s ease

const observer = new IntersectionObserver(entries => {
  entries.forEach(e => { if(e.isIntersecting) e.target.classList.add('visible') })
}, { threshold: 0.12 })
document.querySelectorAll('.reveal, .reveal-left, .reveal-right, .reveal-scale, .stagger')
  .forEach(el => observer.observe(el))

FAQ List

How long does a typical engagement take?
Our flagship Partner Revenue Program runs 12 weeks. By week 12, you have a live partner motion — recruited partners, a working playbook, and active pipeline.
Do we need an existing partner base?
No. We regularly build partner programs from scratch. We handle the partner identification, outreach, and recruitment as part of the engagement.
What kind of companies does Blacfox work with?
Enterprise SaaS companies, typically between Series B and Series D, with between $5M and $50M ARR and an existing direct sales motion they want to leverage through partners.
