01 · Product marketing

What is product marketing for B2B tech companies?

Product marketing is the discipline of making a product easier to buy. Not easier to use — that's product management. Not easier to find — that's demand generation. Easier to buy. That's a different problem — and it's the one Blacfox solves for B2B tech companies.

02 · The real problem

B2B buyers don't purchase what you make.

They purchase what they believe they'll get. The gap between your product and what buyers believe about it is a positioning and messaging problem. We close that gap — with research, not guesswork.

03 · The USP question

What makes a strong B2B USP? It's not a slogan — it's the sharpest line of an enhanced offering.

Most B2B USPs are taglines. They describe features, not outcomes. They're written from the inside out — by people who already know the product — and they land flat with buyers who don't.

A strong B2B USP = customer language + reduced friction + real proof. Anything less is a tagline.
