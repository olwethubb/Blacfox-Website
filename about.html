03 · How we think

Four opinions that shape every B2B tech engagement.

01

Qualified meetings are the outcome. Leads aren't.

Every B2B marketing agency claim is built on lead volume. Leads don't pay invoices. We measure what marketing actually delivered to the sales team — qualified meetings with decision-makers who are ready to talk — and stay involved until that handoff works.

02

Research before everything.

We don't build a PMF from assumptions. We talk to real buyers first — every time. Their language, their trade-offs, their alternatives, their fears. The research is the most expensive step and the most important one.

03

Positioning is the work. Campaigns are the delivery.

Most agencies skip straight to campaigns. We don't. Without positioning, campaigns spend budget re-educating buyers about who you are. With it, every rand works harder from day one.

04

Stay in the room past the lead.

The hand-off from marketing to sales is where most B2B programmes fail. We stay involved — SDR follow-up, meeting support, sales collateral — until the pipeline moves. Not until the campaign ends.
