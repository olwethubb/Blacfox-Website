Common questions

What people ask us before they call.

What is pipeline ACV and how does Blacfox measure it?

Pipeline ACV (Annual Contract Value) is the total annualised value of qualified sales opportunities created by a marketing campaign. Blacfox measures pipeline ACV (not leads, impressions, or MQLs) because it is the metric that reflects actual revenue potential. A 10:1 pipeline ACV ratio means that for every R1 invested in a Blacfox campaign, R10 in qualified pipeline is created. Our portfolio average across 11 engagements (May 2025–May 2026) is 10:1, against a B2B industry benchmark of approximately 3:1.

What is a B2B growth consultancy, and how is it different from a marketing agency?

A B2B growth consultancy combines positioning strategy, content, performance marketing, and SDR (sales development) into a single outcome-led programme, and measures success on qualified pipeline, not activity. A traditional marketing agency typically hands over leads and ends its involvement. Blacfox stays involved through the entire journey: from buyer research and messaging to campaign execution, SDR follow-up, and sales-ready meetings. The key difference is accountability, we guarantee 10:1 pipeline ACV on your investment or we keep working until we hit it.

Which companies does Blacfox work with?

Blacfox works exclusively with B2B technology companies. Our primary clients are Microsoft and SAP partners: the resellers, implementers, and managed service providers competing in the tech channel. We also work with ISVs (Independent Software Vendors) and software vendors that need to generate demand and enable their partner channel. We do not work with B2C companies or generalist tech firms outside the B2B ecosystem.

How does Blacfox's performance-based pricing work?

Blacfox operates on an outcomes-based pricing model. Clients pay on results, specifically on qualified pipeline ACV generated, not on hours worked or activities completed. If the agreed pipeline target is not reached within the campaign window, Blacfox continues the programme at no additional cost until it is. This model reflects our confidence in the methodology and aligns our incentives directly with our clients' revenue goals.

Does Blacfox handle Microsoft co-op funding campaigns?

Yes. Blacfox manages Microsoft co-op funded campaigns end-to-end for Microsoft partners, from eligibility assessment and campaign planning through to Proof of Execution (PoE) documentation and ROI reporting. We have experience navigating Microsoft's changing quarterly priorities and ensure campaigns comply with co-op requirements so partners maximise their available funding without rejected claims.
